Savitri Devi, 34
Tailoring Business Owner — Nizamabad, Telangana
Started with a single sewing machine and a ₹50,000 Mudra loan. Today she runs a boutique with 5 employees and earns ₹45,000/month. "I never imagined I could be a job creator," she says with pride.
💡 Used: Mudra Yojana + PMKVY Training
"
Rekha Kumari, 28
Online Tutor — Warangal, Telangana
A class 10 dropout who learned computer basics through PMKVY. She now teaches 40 students online via video calls, earning ₹18,000/month from home while caring for her two children.
💡 Used: PMKVY Digital Course + Jan Dhan Account
"
Lakshmi Bai, 45
Handicraft Exporter — Medak, Telangana
Used PM Vishwakarma Yojana to get certified and received a ₹15,000 toolkit. Her bamboo craft products now sell on Amazon and Flipkart. Monthly income grew from ₹3,000 to ₹40,000 in just 2 years.
💡 Used: PM Vishwakarma Yojana + Digital Marketing
